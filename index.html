I am an Asst. Professor at Department of Computer Science, at Tuskegee University (TU). I obtained my PhD degree from the Center for Data-intensive Systems (Daisy), Department of Computer Science, Aalborg University Denmark. Before that, I received my Masters in Technologies in E-Government from , University of Trento, Italy and Masters in Computer Science and Engineering from Chalmers Technology University, Sweden .
